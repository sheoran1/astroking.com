Delete Your Account - AstroKing

If you wish to delete your AstroKing account and all associated data, you can request deletion through the app or by contacting us directly.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. In-App Option: Go to Profile tab > Delete Account to submit a deletion request or follow the instructions to contact us.
  2. Email Option: Email us at hello@astroking.com from the email address linked to your account with the subject line: “Delete My Account - AstroKing”. Include your username or User ID (found in Settings).
  3. We may request additional information to verify your identity, such as confirming your email address.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

Upon receiving and verifying your request, we will:

  • Delete your personal data (e.g., name, email, phone, preferences).
  • Delete all app usage data associated with your account.
  • Remove your account from our system within 14 business days.
  • Automatically delete payment and transaction records within 90 days to comply with legal and accounting requirements (e.g., tax regulations or chargeback processing).
  • Instruct third-party providers to delete your personal data where applicable.
  • Retain anonymized usage data that cannot be linked to your identity.

You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data are deleted. If your request is incomplete, we will contact you to provide the necessary information.
